Questions? Answers.

Will it just give me the answers?

No — and that's the point. It gives hints and asks questions so you get there yourself. That's how it sticks for the test.

Do I need to download anything?

Nope. It works right here in your browser. Just allow the microphone when it asks.

What ages is it for?

Students 12–18. Tell the tutor your grade and it adjusts how it explains things.

Is my voice recorded?

Your conversation is processed by our voice AI partner (ElevenLabs) to make the tutor work. Don't share personal stuff like your full name, school, or address.
